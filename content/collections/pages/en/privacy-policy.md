By accessing or browsing our websites, registering for and/or attending our events (in person or virtually), you confirm that you have read, understood, and agree to this privacy policy. We are committed to ensuring the privacy and security of your personal information.

          2 Information We Collect

          We may collect the following types of personal information:

          2.1.1 Contact Information
          This includes your name, email address, phone or mobile number, company name, designation, nationality, country of residence, DOB.

          2.1.2 Event Registration Information
          When you register for an event, we may collect information such as your event preferences, previous attendances, encounters, and choice of categories. If you register for an event using your social media profile, we will capture the information made available by your social media provider in accordance with the privacy settings configured via that platform.

          2.1.3 Payment Information
          If you purchase tickets or event services through our platform, or through our authorised third-party platforms, we may collect payment card information, billing address, and transaction details. Please note that we do not store payment card information; it is processed securely through third-party payment processors.

          2.1.4 Document Uploads
          If you attend our defence exhibitions, we may ask for your passport, UAE resident ID and personal passport size picture. The documents are collected to ensure security clearance is issued to accept the attendee to attend the event. These documents are stored in the cloud servers of AWS.

          2.1.5 Communication Data
          We may collect and store communications you have with us, including emails and messages sent through our website or mobile apps.

          2.1.6 Technical Information
          We may collect information about your device and how you use our website or mobile app, including IP address, browser type, and operating system.

          2.1.7 Cookies and Tracking Technologies
          We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to collect information about your browsing activities on our website. You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings.

          2.1.8 Media Personnel Registration
          If you are a member of the media we may collect information about your proof of media. Proof of media is a document upload attached on the registration form, the document could be but not limited to a media or trade license of an individual or entity or a media id authorised by a listed media company.

          3 How We Use Your Information
          The personal information you submit to us may be used for the following purposes:

          3.1.1 Recording an interest in an event
          Your interest in an event is recorded via a form on the respective event website. This form typically has (but is not limited to) the following form fields – Full Name, Email Address, Phone number, Comments, Company name and Job Title. The interest form is common to those with an interest to exhibit or visit the event.

          3.1.2 Event Registration
          To process your event registration and provide you access to attend the event.

          3.1.3 Customer Support
          To respond to your inquiries, provide customer support, and resolve issues.

          3.1.4 Marketing and Promotions
          To send you event updates, newsletters, and promotional materials, which you can opt out of at any time.

          3.1.5 Analytics
          We collect information such as the pages you visit, the links you click on, the searches you perform, and the device and browser you are using. We may also collect information about your IP address and location.
          We use this collected information for analytics purposes. It is to improve the user experience, and to identify and develop new features and products, to understand how visitors use our website, also to enhance and track trends and patterns.
          We use a variety of analytics tools, including Google Analytics, to collect and analyse this information. We do not share your personal information with third-party analytics providers without your consent.

          3.1.6 Payment Processing
          To process payments for event tickets and services.

          4 How We Share Your Information
          We may share your personal information with third parties in the following circumstances:

          4.1.1 Service Providers
          We may share your information with third-party service providers who assist us in delivering our services, such as event registration companies, payment processors, marketing agencies, and IT providers. Such service providers are contractually bound to only process your information in accordance with our instructions and in line with this Privacy Policy.

          4.1.2 Legal Compliance
          We may disclose your information to comply with legal obligations or protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property.

          4.1.3 Event Partners
          If you attend an event organised in partnership with other organisations, we may share your information with those partners for event coordination purposes.

          4.1.4 ADNEC Group Marketing
          We may share your information with ADNEC Group’s Marketing Department, so that they can send you updates, newsletters, and promotional materials regarding other ADNEC Group companies and
          services.
          ADNEC Group Marketing will never share your information with any third parties but may occasionally send you marketing communications on behalf of third parties. You can opt out of ADNEC Group communications at any time.

          5 Data Security
          We take appropriate technical and organisational security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, destruction, accidental or intentional manipulation or loss. However, no data transmission over the internet or stored data can be guaranteed to be 100%
          secure.

          6 Your Data Rights

          6.1.1 Access and Correction
          You can access and update your personal information by logging into your account on our website or mobile app, where one is created for an event.

          6.1.2 Opt-Out
          You can opt out of receiving marketing communications from us or ADNEC Group Marketing by following the unsubscribe instructions in the emails we send or by contacting us directly.

          7 Children’s Privacy
          Capital Events services are not intended for individuals under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

          8 Changes to this Privacy Policy
          We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal and regulatory reasons. We will notify you of any significant changes through our website.

          9 The Data Controller
          The Data Controller is Abu Dhabi National Exhibitions Company PJSC, whose registered address is ADNEC Group Head Office, Abu Dhabi National Exhibition Centre, P.O. Box 5546, Khaleej Al Arabi St, Abu Dhabi, UAE

          10 Contact Us
          If you have any further queries regarding how we process your data, please send an e-mail to dataprotectionofficer@adnec.ae

          11 Acceptance of Terms
          By using our services, you signify your acceptance of this Privacy Policy.

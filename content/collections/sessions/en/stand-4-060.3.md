<p>Workshop Objective: highlight the importance of the Arabic language in strengthening identity and belonging, supporting children’s cognitive and emotional development, and encouraging home and educational best practices that promote language learning in the early years. Workshop Themes:</p><p>Language development for children: when and how does it happen? Language as a tool for building identity and belonging.</p><p>Challenges of learning Arabic in a multilingual environment. The family’s role in expanding vocabulary and language comprehension.</p><p>Practical activities to promote language in daily routines (reading,</p><p>play, conversation)</p>

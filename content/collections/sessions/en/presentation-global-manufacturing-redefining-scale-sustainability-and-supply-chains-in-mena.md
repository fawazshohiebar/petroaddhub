<p>Multinational companies play a pivotal role in bridging global frameworks with local priorities — whether in sustainability, innovation, nutrition, or responsible advocacy. This panel explores how global learnings can be localized to strengthen the UAE’s policy ecosystem, drive innovation, and enable public–private collaboration. It focuses on the intersection of global corporate strategy, local adaptation, and</p><p>partnership with government — essential to shaping regulations that support both competitiveness and sustainability.</p>

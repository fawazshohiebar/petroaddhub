<p>How we talk about food shapes how people eat it. Public debate on novel foods often swings between hype and fear. To build consumer trust, the story must be about taste, affordability and everyday life, not labs or jargon. This session explores how media, entertainment and education can reframe the narrative, helping families see alternative proteins as safe, familiar and desirable.</p>

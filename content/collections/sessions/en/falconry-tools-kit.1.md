<p>Objective</p><p>Introduce participants to traditional falconry tools and their uses</p><p>Concept</p><p>Participants will be part of a guided exploration and learn about the different tools used for falconry such as the wakir, mikhlat, burqaa, manqala, mursal, Tulwah and more. Participants will learn about the use and purpose of each tool and prepare their falconry kit and place their tools in the bag to take it with them in their journey</p>

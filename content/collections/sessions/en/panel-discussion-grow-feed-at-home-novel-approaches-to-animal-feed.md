<p>Home-grown feed is a cornerstone of resilience. From insect meal to algae oils and microbial proteins, alternative feeds can reduce import dependence, stabilise costs and strengthen local farming. This session explores how the UAE can turn innovation into viable feed projects, setting standards, structuring offtake, and supporting farmers to create a stronger foundation for national food security.</p>

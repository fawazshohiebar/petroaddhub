<p>Workshop Objective:</p><p>We aim to highlight the importance of the family’s role in instilling Emirati values in children through conscious parenting and everyday situations, while connecting cultural values to family experiences. Workshop Themes:</p><p>An engaging, interactive session that helps parents integrate core values such as respect and responsibility through family activities that encourage dialogue and understanding with children.</p><p>•What are the essential Emirati values children should learn?</p><p>•The relation between national identity and family upbringing</p><p>•Family activities that nurture values such as respect, generosity, responsibility, and belonging.</p><p>•The role of traditional stories and heritage in shaping children’s character.</p><p>•Interactive exercises using sensory experiences and symbolic play</p><p>to instill values.</p><p>Target Audience:</p><p>Parents, caregivers, and professionals interested in promoting Emirati identity in children</p><p>-Duration: 30 minutes</p><p>-Live session conducted in the auditorium within the Academy’s stand</p>

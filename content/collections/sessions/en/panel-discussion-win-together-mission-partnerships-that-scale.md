<p>Collaboration accelerates solutions. Challenge programmes and mission-driven partnerships can unite start-ups, corporates, and governments around shared bottlenecks - from yields and fats to shelf life. This session explores how Abu Dhabi is driving global design challenges that deliver practical breakthroughs, standardised test methods, and shared infrastructure, ensuring winning ideas scale beyond the demonstration stage.</p>

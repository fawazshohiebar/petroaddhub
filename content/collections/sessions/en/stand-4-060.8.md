<p>Activity Objective:</p><p>To introduce children to the Emirati art of Talli embroidery, </p><p>instill cultural identity and creative expression, and </p><p>promote values of empathy and belonging.</p><p>Concept:</p><p>An interactive workshop titled “Talli Bracelet,” where </p><p>children learn about the Emirati Talli craft and Arabic </p><p>letters, then design their own bracelets using colorful </p><p>threads and beads that incorporate letters or symbols </p><p>reflecting their values.</p><p></p>

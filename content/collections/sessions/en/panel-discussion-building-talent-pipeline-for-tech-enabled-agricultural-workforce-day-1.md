The future of agriculture is digital—from precision farming and drones to data analytics, AI, and smart irrigation systems. As the industry rapidly evolves, the biggest challenge isn't just adopting new technologies—it's ensuring we have a skilled workforce ready to use them. This panel explores how we can build a robust, future-ready talent pipeline to power the next era of agriculture.

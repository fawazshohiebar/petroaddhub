<p>Workshop Objective:</p><p>To raise children’s awareness of the importance of falcons </p><p>in Emirati cultural heritage and to nurture their appreciation of traditional heritage through a creative, interactive activity. Concept: A themed arts and crafts workshop titled “Little Falcons,”</p><p>where children participate in hands-on activities such as </p><p>making falcon masks or decorating and coloring falcon </p><p>wings using simple, safe materials. The session combines </p><p>learning and play in a fun environment.</p>

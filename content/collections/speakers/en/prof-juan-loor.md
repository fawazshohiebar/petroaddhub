Dr. Juan Loor is Professor of Animal Sciences and Division of Nutritional Sciences at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign, recognized globally for pioneering research in ruminant nutrigenomics and metabolic physiology. His research spans over 25 countries, with more than 90 invited international lectures addressing ruminant physiology, nutrition, and climate-resilient livestock systems.

Dr. Loor has built an extensive international network through collaborations and visiting professorships spanning Asia, Europe, and the Americas. He has mentored more than 100 graduate students and postdoctoral fellows worldwide, many of whom now hold academic and industry leadership roles in their home countries. His leadership has fostered faculty and student exchanges, joint symposia, and scientific writing workshops that strengthen global research capacity.

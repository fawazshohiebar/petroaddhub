Daniel Bisley is the Chief Financial Officer of Vivici, a biotechnology company pioneering dairy protein production through precision fermentation. In 2025, he led the successful completion of a €32.5 million Series A funding round, supporting the company’s strategic growth and innovation agenda. Prior to this, he served as CFO to various high-growth ventures, advising on capital raising and strategic transactions across global markets.

Daniel previously held the CFO role at SO Connect, a leading SaaS platform for local businesses, where he secured venture and debt financing and led a strategic exit. 

Before transitioning to operational leadership, Daniel built a strong foundation in finance through roles in venture capital at a TMT-focused investment fund, private equity at The Carlyle Group, and investment banking at Merrill Lynch in London, where he advised on mergers and acquisitions across the Benelux region.

Daniel holds an MSc in Finance and Investments (Cum Laude) and a BSc in International Business Administration from RSM Erasmus University.

Dina Mongy is the Head of Corporate Affairs and Sustainability for Coastal 
Arabia at Nestlé, where she drives government relations, advocacy, 
communications and sustainability initiatives across the UAE, Kuwait, and 
Qatar. She works closely with government entities, industry associations, 
and key stakeholders to shape policies and lead discussions on critical 
topics such as circular economy, regulation frameworks, climate action, 
and responsible business practices.

With extensive experience in public policy, corporate affairs, and 
sustainability, Dina has a strong track record of engaging with different 
stakeholders, shaping industry regulations, and fostering strategic 
partnerships. Before joining Nestlé, she served as Head of Government 
Relations and Public Policy at ByteDance, where she led high-level 
government engagements across the region. She was also Associate 
Director and Cluster Coordinator for the Middle East, GCC, and Levant at 
a regional consultancy, supporting multinational companies in their public 
affairs strategies. Additionally, she worked on trade and investment 
relations at the Embassy of Brazil in Cairo, strengthening bilateral 
partnerships.

Dina holds a Bachelor’s degree in Economics from the American 
University in Cairo and a Master’s degree in International Relations (with 
Merit) from Royal Holloway, University of London. 
She has a Business Sustainability Management Certificate from 
Cambridge University and a Public Policy Analysis Certificate from the 
London School of Economics & Political Science.

A multilingual speaker, Dina is fluent in Arabic, English, and French and 
has an intermediate proficiency in Spanish and Portuguese. She is a 
sought-after speaker on public affairs, sustainability, corporate 
responsibility, and policy advocacy, regularly engaging in industry 
dialogues and conferences

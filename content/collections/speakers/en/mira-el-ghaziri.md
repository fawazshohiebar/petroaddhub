Mira El Ghaziri is the Managing Director of HealthyPath, a consultancy dedicated to advancing nutrition policy, food sustainability, and research to transform global food systems. She supports public and private sectors in addressing the critical connection between nutrition security and food security by providing advisory on food innovations and food policies.

A strong believer in the power of collaboration, Mira champions inclusive and impactful dialogues to drive a systemic change in the food environment. She has facilitated critical national food security and nutrition dialogues, bringing key stakeholders together, on healthy sustainable food consumption patterns, and promoting agrifood tech innovation. She has also contributed to several reports on the potential of food tech, particularly alternative proteins in the GCC region.

With academic background in global health policy, nutrition, and food sustainability, Mira brings deep knowledge and experience in driving equitable food policies. Through HealthyPath, she envisions a world where everyone has access to nutritious and sustainable food choices.
